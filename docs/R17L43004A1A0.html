Our Craft
Guided by the skilled hands of local artisans in a Botswana-based factory, each rough diamond is carefully evaluated and delicately shaped to reach its full potential.

By choosing a natural diamond, you help create positive change, uplifting communities and preserving the rich history of this land.

Established in 1975, Yerushalmi Bros. Diamonds is a global leader in the world of diamond manufacturing.

We've built our reputation on innovation, cutting-edge technology, and a commitment to excellence.

In 2006, with great investment in the pioneering era of the diamond industry in Botswana, we have founded our flagship factory in Gaborone.

Being at the forefront of manufacturing technology in Botswana for over 15 years, we have trained hundreds of local diamond experts who have since become leaders in the industry.

We specialize in Hearts & Arrows Rounds with high light reflection, as well as fancy-shaped diamonds.

As De Beers Sight holders since 1998, we are committed to the highest ethical and professional standards. We adhere to the Best Practice Principles (BPP) and follow the Kimberley Process (KP).

From the pure elegance of white diamonds to the captivating beauty of fancy-colored diamonds, we offer an extensive collection of polished goods.

At Yerushalmi Bros. Diamonds, we believe in the beauty, quality, and integrity of our diamonds.
